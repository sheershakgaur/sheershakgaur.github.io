About Us

We, at Pack~connects , are a team of highly enthusiastic and result oriented Packaging professionals & Trainers who assist the clients in resolution of their packaging requirements and packaging related issues. The aim is to develop "onsite and offisite consulting also for packaging thereby minimizing travel and regular meetings. regular interaction with the client.


We serve our clients as per their requirement, Project to Project basis or on monthly retainer-ship contract (few days in a week/month) or we can also offer customized Packaging services as per the requirement of client. AN Pack~connect has Pan-India operation with our prime location in New Delhi with presence at Noida and Gurgaon


We cater to sectors like, Food & Beverages, Food Chains and Restaurants, FBO Home Delivery Packaging, Exterior profiles and finishes, Chemicals, Agriculture, Automobiles, Pipes & Fittings, Cosmetics , Apparels/Garments, Paints and Adhesives, E-commerce, Retail and Consumer Goods etc. Our charges are most economical as compared to industry standard and it depends on the project requirements.
